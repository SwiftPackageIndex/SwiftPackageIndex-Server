Information for Example Package Maintainers

Are you the author, or a maintainer of Example Package? Here's what you need to know to make your package's page on the Swift Package Index, and your README both show the best information about your package.

Compatibility Badges

You can add shields.io badges to your package's README file. Display your package's compatibility with recent versions of Swift, or with different platforms, or both!
